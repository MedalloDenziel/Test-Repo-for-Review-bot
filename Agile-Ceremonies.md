##  Reflection

### How do Agile ceremonies help with communication and alignment?
* Agile ceremonies provide a consistent heartbeat for the team. They ensure that everyone is working on the most important tasks and that "blockers" are identified early. By having these regular touchpoints, we avoid the "silo effect" where someone works on the wrong thing for days without realizing it.

### Which ceremony do you think is most important for your role, and why?
* For my role in Documentation and Webflow, **Backlog Refinement** is the most important. Because I value clear plans and meticulous preparation, having a ceremony dedicated to clarifying requirements before I start coding ensures I can work with high focus and minimal "surprises."

### Document one change you can make to improve your team collaboration.
* Based on my observations of remote teamwork, I will implement **Asynchronous Updates** as my primary collaboration change. Instead of waiting for a meeting to share progress, I will use my Kanban board and Discord to provide status updates in real-time.

**One specific change I will make:**
When I move a card to "Ready for Review," I will include a short 1-2 sentence summary of what was changed and a link to the specific file or page. This respects my teammates' time by giving them all the context they need to review my work immediately.
