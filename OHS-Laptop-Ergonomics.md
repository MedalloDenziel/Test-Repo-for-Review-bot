# OHS-Laptop-Ergonomics - Denziel Medallo

## Reflection

### What equipment changes can you make to improve your workspace setup?
* I currently work primarily on a laptop. To improve my setup, I can use a laptop stand to bring the screen to eye level and connect an external keyboard and mouse. This prevents me from hunching over the small screen and allows my shoulders to remain relaxed.
### What behavioural changes can you implement to improve posture and reduce strain?
* I will focus on sitting upright with my back supported and my feet flat on the floor. I also plan to implement the "20-20-20 rule": every 20 minutes, I will look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.
### How can you remind yourself to maintain good posture and take breaks throughout the day? 
* I will use Focus Bear to schedule mandatory movement breaks. Having a "hard stop" on my screen will force me to stand up, stretch, and reset my posture, which is much more effective than just trying to remember to do it manually.
