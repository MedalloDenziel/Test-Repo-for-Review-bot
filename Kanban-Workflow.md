# Kanban-Workflow - Denziel Medallo

## Reflection

### How does Kanban help manage priorities and avoid overload?
* Kanban provides a visual "at-a-glance" view of my workload. By using Work In Progress (WIP) limits, it forces me to focus on finishing my current task before pulling a new one from the backlog. This prevents the "panic" of having too many open tasks and ensures that my energy is focused on the highest priority item.

### How can you improve your workflow using Kanban principles?
* I can improve my workflow by being disciplined about moving my cards. I will make it a habit to update my board as soon as I start or finish a task. This creates transparency so my team knows exactly what I am working on without needing to message me, preserving my "Deep Work" time.
