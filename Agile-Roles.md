# Agile-Roles - Denziel Medallo

## Reflection
### What is your role, and how does it contribute to the Agile process?
* My role involves Frontend Webflow development and Technical Documentation. In the Agile process, I contribute by turning design requirements into functional web pages and ensuring that our technical processes are documented. This documentation is vital for "Transparency"—one of the pillars of Agile—as it allows the whole team to understand how our systems work.

### How do your responsibilities overlap or interact with other roles in the team?
* I interact closely with **Product Managers** to understand the requirements for new Webflow pages and with **Developers** to ensure my documentation accurately reflects the codebase. If I find a bug while updating a page, I also interact with **QA** to report and track the issue on our Kanban board.

## Task
### Write a short description of how your role fits into the Agile team.
* I act as the bridge between the product vision and the user's first impression. By maintaining the Webflow frontend and creating clear documentation, I ensure that the team can move fast without losing track of how our features are built and maintained.

### Identify one other role you frequently collaborate with and describe how you can improve teamwork with them.
* I frequently collaborate with **UX Designers**. To improve teamwork with them, I can provide early feedback on the technical feasibility of a design in Webflow. By communicating early if a specific design element is difficult to implement, we can find a simpler, more accessible alternative before we get too far into the development process.
